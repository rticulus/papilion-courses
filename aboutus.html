What do we do?

HTK Sverige is a community arts project hailing from H채sselby-V채llingby in Stockholm. In our work, we are based on the philosophy of hip hop, where peace, love and community are at the center. We use a liberating and participatory pedagogy that gives everyone the opportunity to be co-creators in the creative process. Together we explore and tell personal and shared stories by integrating theater with hip hop's various art forms - dance, MCing, graffiti and DJing. Practical and visual methods and tools allow people with different conditions and backgrounds to participate on equal terms. We see hip-hop as a medium for expressing themselves, but also a tool for understanding oneself and others. HTK is led by a group of creative, innovative and experienced artists, artists and educators. We collaborate with Kulturhuset-Stadsteatern in V채llingby and many of our activities take place in their premises. We work primarily in Swedish and English, but the spoken language should never be an obstacle to participation. There are many ways to express yourself and communicate! HTK Sverige is a partnership consisting of three organizations: Community Bridge, Papilion AB and Kulturhuset Stadsteatern V채llingby. The project is funded by the Cultural Council.
